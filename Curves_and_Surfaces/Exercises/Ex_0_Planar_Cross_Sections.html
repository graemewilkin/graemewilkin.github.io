Visualising hyperboloids and spheres using planar cross-sections

Back to Curves and Surfaces homepage

The following links will take you to pictures visualising the intersection of various surfaces (the sphere and the hyperboloids of one and two sheets) with different planes. We can often describe these curves of intersection algebraically (and you did this in Q5 from Exercise Sheet 0), and this will help to build up a picture of the surface. The pictures in the links below will show you how your algebraic proof matches the 3D picture of the surface, which should help your geometric intuition about these planar cross-sections.
