The following picture shows the graph of \(y=x^2\) in the \(xy\) plane together with its osculating circle. The point \(O\) is the centre of the osculating circle. You can click and drag the slider to move the osculating circle along the curve.
The following picture shows the graph of \(y=x^3\) in the \(xy\) plane together with its osculating circle. Again, you can click and drag the slider to move the point along the curve. Note that the osculating circle changes sides after it passes through the point of inflection. This is an example of the signed curvature changing sign.
