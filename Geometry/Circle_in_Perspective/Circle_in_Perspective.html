Drawing a circle in perspective

The red circle is inscribed inside the square ABCD. The points A, B, C, D project to the points W, X, Y, Z respectively on the xy plane, using P as a point of perspective. The red circle projects to the orange ellipse.
In these pages we will explore the question of how to construct the ellipse.
You can click and drag to move the point of perspective P. The circle lies on the plane through Q, R and S, and you can change this plane by moving these points. The circle has centre O and radius OF, and you can move these points to change the circle. The points A, B, C, D and W, X, Y, Z will automatically be redrawn. You can rotate the 3D figure by clicking and dragging the box.
