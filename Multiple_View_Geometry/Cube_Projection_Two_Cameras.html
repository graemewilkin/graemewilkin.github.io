Two camera projection of a cube

Back to Multiple View Geometry homepage


In the following picture, a cube is projected onto two different planes using the points \(C_1\) and \(C_2\) as camera centres. This extends the single camera projection of a cube that we saw earlier.

You can click and drag the camera centres \(C_1\) and \(C_2\) to move them around the picture. Notice how the two different projections give more information about the original three dimensional object.
