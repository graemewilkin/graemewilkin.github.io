Directions to University of York Maths Department


The maths department is in James College on the west campus. You can search for the location on the University campus map (this includes a link to an interactive campus map).
The university (about 1 mile SE) and rail station (about 0.5 miles NW) are on opposite sides of the city centre.

Bus.
Exit the station and catch bus #66 or #67. These buses come approximately every 10 minutes, take 17 minutes to reach the campus, and cost approximately £2 for a one-way ticket (pay the driver). Exact change is not necessary, and you can use your credit card.
Once the bus reaches campus, the best stop is the "University Library" stop near the first footbridge across the road. Then cross the road, walk downhill, and cross the lake.

Taxi.
There is a taxi rank at the station. A taxi should cost about £10 and take 10-15 minutes.

Walking.
Walking to campus through the city centre takes about 40 minutes from the rail station.

Car. Pay and Display parking is marked on the campus map. If you are planning to arrive by car then please contact us to arrange a visitor's permit.
